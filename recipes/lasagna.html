Crab Lasagna

Description

A delightful seafood twist on the classic Italian lasagna, featuring succulent crab meat.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Crab meat
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Alfredo sauce
  • Garlic
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  2. Mix crab meat with ricotta and seasonings
  3. Layer noodles, crab mixture, and cheeses
  4. Pour Alfredo sauce between layers
  5. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes
