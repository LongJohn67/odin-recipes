Spicy Crunchy Noodles

Description

A delicious Asian-inspired dish featuring crispy noodles with a spicy kick!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz thin wheat noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup crushed peanuts
  • Red pepper flakes to taste

Steps

  1. Boil noodles according to package instructions until al dente
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium-high heat
  3. Add boiled noodles to the hot pan and fry until crispy
  4. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for 1 minute
  5. Pour in soy sauce, chili oil, and sesame oil
  6. Toss noodles until well coated and crispy
  7. Top with green onions and crushed peanuts
  8. Sprinkle red pepper flakes to taste
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
