Classic Pizza Recipe

Description

A simple yet delicious homemade pizza recipe that will rival your favorite pizzeria!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, sugar, and yeast in a large bowl
  2. Add olive oil and warm water, knead until smooth
  3. Let dough rise for 1 hour
  4. Roll out dough and add toppings
  5. Bake at 450Â°F for 15-20 minutes
